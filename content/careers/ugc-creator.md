# UGC Creator (Brand Content)

Hook: Help brands feel human—useful, honest, and watchable content.

## What You Do
You create product demos and short-form videos that brands can run as organic posts or ads.

### Daily Work
- Film product demos and honest reviews
- Edit short-form videos with clear hooks
- Respond to briefs and deliver raw + edited files
- Collaborate with brands on messaging

### Deliverables
- 3–10 short-form videos per package
- Raw footage + edited cuts
- Caption versions and thumbnails
- Usage rights agreements

## Who This Is Good For
- Creators comfortable on camera
- Editors who like fast iteration

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Filming basics; lighting and audio
- Editing short-form videos
- Storytelling and hooks
- Simple scripting and outlines

### Soft Skills
- Communication and reliability
- Positive, helpful tone
- Professionalism with deadlines

## Tools You’ll Use
- Camera/phone, mic, lights
- Editing tools (CapCut, Premiere)
- Docs for briefs and revisions

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Film 20 short-form demos for 5 products
- Build a portfolio reel and case studies
- Practice hooks and captions

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Create packages with clear deliverables
- Pitch 20 brands with helpful ideas and test edit
- Publish testimonial-driven results

### 60–90 Days (Client-Ready)
- Offer sprint packages (content + captions + thumbnails)
- Refine pricing and usage rights
- Build referrals engine

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Demo reel with metrics
- Product comparison series
- Hook experiments with retention outcomes
- Caption template library

## How to Get Your First Client/Job
1. Create demos for products you own
2. Publish reels and metrics honestly
3. Pitch small brands with ideas and test video
4. Offer clear packages and timelines

## Pricing & Salary Ranges (No Guarantees)
- Packages: $300–$2,500 depending on scope and rights
- Ad-ready sets priced higher with usage terms

> Disclaimer: Rates depend on scope, rights, niche, and your proof of performance.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Overly salesy → Keep tone helpful and honest
- Messy footage → Improve lighting/audio; build SOPs
- Weak hooks → Practice opening lines; get to the point fast

## FAQs
- Do I need a fancy camera? A good phone + mic is enough.
- Who owns the content? Define usage rights in your contract.
- How to price? Scope + rights + your proof of outcomes.
