# Sales Development Rep (Remote SDR)

Hook: Create pipeline reliably—research, outreach, and consistency.

## What You Do
You find qualified prospects and book meetings. You research ideal customers, craft relevant messages, and follow up professionally.

### Daily Work
- Build prospect lists and research accounts
- Write outreach emails and messages
- Book meetings and manage calendar
- Track metrics: opens, replies, meetings, pipeline

### Deliverables
- Target account lists
- Outreach templates and scripts
- Pipeline dashboard and reporting

## Who This Is Good For
- Persistent communicators with process discipline
- People comfortable with rejection and iteration

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- ICP definition and account research
- Cold email and messaging frameworks
- CRM usage and pipeline tracking

### Soft Skills
- Resilience and positive tone
- Organization and follow-up rigor
- Curiosity and helpful attitude

## Tools You’ll Use
- CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Outreach tools and inbox
- LinkedIn and data providers

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Define ICP and draft 10 templates
- Send 300 emails; test variants
- Track responses and meetings

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Build case study: meetings booked and learnings
- Improve templates; personalize at scale
- Collaborate with marketing for content

### 60–90 Days (Job-Ready)
- Publish outcomes; ask for referrals
- Interview prep with mock calls
- Show your process and persistence

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Outreach system with dashboards
- Template library with test results
- Case study: booked meetings and pipeline

## How to Get Your First Role
1. Do outreach for a small business
2. Publish metrics and case study
3. Apply with proof and process

## Compensation (No Guarantees)
- Employment: $40k–$65k base; variable compensation common

> Disclaimer: Variable pay is common; outcomes depend on market, offer, and execution.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Spray-and-pray → Personalize; test; measure
- Weak follow-up → Schedule cadences and reminders
- No ICP clarity → Define tightly and revisit

## FAQs
- Is it all cold email? Mix channels; be helpful.
- Is rejection common? Yes—stay calm and iterate.
- How to advance? Improve process; move to AE roles.
