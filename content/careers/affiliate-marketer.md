# Affiliate Marketer / Niche Site Builder

Hook: Create genuinely helpful content and earn when readers buy with confidence.

## What You Do
You research keywords, write helpful articles, and monetize with affiliate programs.

## Day in the Life
- Morning: research 5–10 keywords; outline 1–2 posts
- Midday: write drafts; add internal links and visuals
- Afternoon: publish and track clicks; update older posts
- Weekly: review RPMs, CTR, and rankings; plan next cluster

### Daily Work
- Keyword research and content planning
- Writing reviews, comparisons, and guides
- On-page SEO and internal linking
- Tracking clicks and revenue; iterating content

### Deliverables
- Topic cluster plan
- 20–50 helpful articles over months
- Monetization setup and tracking
- Quarterly update plan

Extended deliverables:
- Review templates (pros/cons, comparison tables, FAQs)
- Link placement strategy (contextual links, disclosure, trust badges)
- CTR improvement briefs (title/thumbnail experiments)
- Newsletter plan for retention and trust

## Who This Is Good For
- Patient builders who enjoy compounding results
- Writers who like research and helpful content

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Keyword research and topical clusters
- On-page SEO and internal linking
- Affiliate program setup and tracking

### Soft Skills
- Consistency and long-term thinking
- Honesty and helpful-first mindset
- Editorial operations

## Tools You’ll Use
- Keyword tools (Ahrefs, Semrush)
- CMS (WordPress/Webflow)
- Analytics (GSC, GA4)
- Affiliate platforms (Amazon, Impact, CJ)

Optional: schema generators for Product/Review, and basic image editing for comparisons.

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Pick a niche; map 50 keywords
- Write 10 helpful posts; set internal links
- Apply to affiliate programs

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Publish 20 more posts; add comparison tables
- Improve CTR with angles and thumbnails
- Track clicks and earnings

### 60–90 Days (Expansion-Ready)
- Publish supporting guides and updates
- Build an email list; nurture with value
- Create 2 “best-of” pillar pages

Add one monetization test: switch link placements from footers to contextual sections; measure CTR and earnings.

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Topic cluster with 20 posts
- Review + comparison series
- CTR improvement case study
- Helpful hub pages

Acceptance criteria examples:
- Cluster: hub interlinks all supports; new posts rank for long-tails
- Reviews: include real photos/tests; honest pros/cons; clear disclosure
- CTR case study: improved titles/images; measurable click increases

## How to Get Your First Income
1. Choose a niche with clear commercial intent
2. Publish consistently for 3–6 months
3. Earn first commissions; reinvest in quality
4. Optimize with data and user feedback

### Monetization Models
- Display ads (RPMs vary widely)
- Affiliate links (contextual, comparison tables)
- Email list and simple guides/products later

## Income Ranges (No Guarantees)
- $100–$2,000+/mo common after months of consistent publishing
- Highly variable; depends on rankings and conversion

> Disclaimer: No guaranteed income. Results depend on niche, rankings, and consistency.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Thin reviews → Add tests, photos, pros/cons, FAQs
- No internal links → Build clusters; link systematically
- Chasing trends → Stick to helpful evergreen topics

Additional:
- Overly salesy writing → Be honest; readers remember trust
- Ignoring disclosures → Be transparent; it’s good business and policy

## FAQs
- How long till income? Often months; compounding over time.
- Is the niche important? Yes—choose one with stable demand.
- Can I outsource writing? Yes—maintain quality control.
- Do I need backlinks? Helpful assets earn links; focus on usefulness
- Should I add video? If it helps the review—test and measure
