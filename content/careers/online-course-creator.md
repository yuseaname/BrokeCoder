# Online Course Creator

Hook: Turn your skills into helpful lessons—student-first design, honest outcomes.

## What You Do
You design curriculum, produce lessons, and launch courses focused on real outcomes.

### Daily Work
- Plan curriculum and lesson briefs
- Script, record, and edit lessons
- Build worksheets and projects
- Set up platform and payments

### Deliverables
- Curriculum map and lesson outlines
- Recorded lessons + resources
- Launch plan and email sequence
- Student success tracking

## Who This Is Good For
- Teachers at heart; patient and practical
- Builders comfortable with video and structure

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Curriculum design and scaffolding
- Video/audio production basics
- Marketing (email + landing pages)
- Student support and community

### Soft Skills
- Clarity and empathy
- Honest communication; no hype
- Iteration and listening

## Tools You’ll Use
- Platforms: Teachable, Kajabi, Podia
- Video tools: Resolve, Descript
- Docs/Notion: curriculum and tracking

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Define student outcome and scope
- Outline 10–20 lessons with projects
- Record 3 sample lessons

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Build landing page + email sequence
- Record core lessons; edit cleanly
- Run beta cohort for feedback

### 60–90 Days (Launch-Ready)
- Publish course and support students
- Iterate with outcomes and testimonials
- Plan V2 improvements

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Mini-course (3–5 lessons) with outcomes
- Landing page with honest messaging
- Lesson demo reel with student feedback

## How to Get Your First Sales
1. Teach free workshops; earn trust
2. Offer a mini-course; collect feedback
3. Launch pre-sales with clear outcomes
4. Ship core lessons; support learners

## Income Ranges (No Guarantees)
- Highly variable: a few hundred to several thousand per launch

> Disclaimer: No guaranteed income. Focus on outcomes and honest delivery.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Overscoping → Start smaller; launch and iterate
- Hypey messaging → Be honest; show outcomes and effort
- Messy production → Build templates; keep style consistent

## FAQs
- Do I need a big audience? No—start small; teach genuinely.
- How long are courses? Scope to outcomes; avoid filler.
- Refunds? Respect policies; aim for student success.
