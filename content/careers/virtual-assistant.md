# Virtual Assistant (Tech-Forward VA)

Hook: Keep projects moving—organized systems, helpful communication, steady delivery.

## What You Do
You support creators and teams with research, scheduling, content prep, and operations.

### Daily Work
- Manage calendars and scheduling
- Prepare content assets and posts
- Research topics and compile briefs
- Track tasks and SOPs

### Deliverables
- SOPs and checklists
- Content calendars and asset pipelines
- Research docs and summaries
- Weekly status reports

## Who This Is Good For
- Organized communicators who like systems
- Helpful builders who enjoy multi-tasking

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Docs/Notion, Sheets, calendar tools
- Basic content posting + formatting
- Research and summarization

### Soft Skills
- Reliability and responsiveness
- Clear communication and prioritization
- Process improvement mindset

## Tools You’ll Use
- Notion/Docs/Sheets
- Scheduling and inbox tools
- Social + CMS platforms

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Build templates and SOPs for common tasks
- Support a small project end-to-end
- Track metrics and outcomes

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Ship 3 client SOP packs
- Publish helpful case studies (organization wins)
- Improve speed and reliability

### 60–90 Days (Client-Ready)
- Offer a “VA Starter Pack” service
- Collect testimonials and referrals
- Productize monthly retainers

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- SOP library for content + ops
- Research + briefs with outcomes
- Calendar + asset pipeline setup

## How to Get Your First Client/Job
1. Help creators with clear offers
2. Deliver consistently for 1–2 months
3. Ask for testimonials and referrals

## Pricing & Salary Ranges (No Guarantees)
- Employment: $35k–$55k entry; $45k–$70k mid-level
- Retainers: $400–$1,800/mo depending on scope

> Disclaimer: Income depends on scope, reliability, and outcomes.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Vague deliverables → Define SOPs and metrics
- Overcommitting → Set boundaries and schedules
- No documentation → Write processes; share updates

## FAQs
- Do I need niche expertise? Helpful, but reliability matters most.
- How to price? By scope—hours only as a last resort.
- How to grow? Productize services and improve outcomes.
