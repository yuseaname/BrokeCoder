# Copywriter (Direct Response)

Hook: Turn attention into action with clear, persuasive writing.

## What You Do
You write copy that sells: landing pages, ad creatives, email sequences, and headlines that move people to act.

### Daily Work
- Research offers, audience pain points, and competitors
- Draft headlines, hooks, CTAs, and product positioning
- Write landing pages, ads, scripts, and emails
- Test variations and refine based on performance
- Collaborate with designers and performance marketers

### Deliverables
- Landing page copy (hero, benefits, offer, FAQ)
- Ad copy variants with hooks and angles
- Email sequences (welcome, launch, nurture)
- Offer positioning and value proposition docs

## Who This Is Good For
- Clear thinkers who love solving persuasion puzzles
- Writers who enjoy research and testing
- People comfortable with performance metrics

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Direct response frameworks (AIDA, PAS, 4U, PASTOR)
- Offer design and positioning
- CRO basics and landing page structure
- Email and ad copy structure

### Soft Skills
- Research discipline and curiosity
- Client communication and feedback loops
- Iteration mindset: test, learn, improve

## Tools You’ll Use
- Docs/Notion for briefs and drafts
- Email platforms (ConvertKit, Klaviyo, Mailchimp)
- Ad managers (Meta Ads, Google Ads)
- Analytics: GA4, heatmaps, A/B testing tools

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Study 30 high-performing landing pages
- Rewrite 10 pages with better structure and hooks
- Draft 3 email sequences for a hypothetical offer

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Build a “copy lab” with briefs, swipe files, and templates
- Create a case study: A/B tested headlines + CTR lift
- Offer a free “headline pack” to 3 small businesses

### 60–90 Days (Client-Ready)
- Pitch 30 businesses with tailored offer audits
- Deliver landing page + email sequence pilot
- Collect testimonials, permission to share outcomes

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Landing page + split test with lift documented
- Email sequence (5–7 emails) with open/click metrics
- Ad creative variants with CTR comparison
- Offer audit + positioning document

## How to Get Your First Client/Job
1. Pick one niche (coaches, SaaS, local services)
2. Build 3 strong case studies (even hypothetical with data logic)
3. Offer a pilot package (landing page + headline pack)
4. Deliver quickly and ask for testimonials
5. Upsell to email + ad copy retainers

## Pricing & Salary Ranges (No Guarantees)
- Employment: $50k–$85k entry; $70k–$110k mid-level
- Freelance: $800–$3,500 landing pages; $300–$1,500 email sequences
- Retainers: $1,000–$4,000/mo for ongoing copy + testing

> Disclaimer: Outcomes depend on offer quality, traffic, niche, and testing. No guarantees.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Fluffy copy → Focus on clarity, benefits, and proof
- No research → Interview customers; pull language from real feedback
- One draft only → Produce variants; test headlines and CTAs
- Ignoring mobile → Mobile-first layout and scanning behavior

## FAQs
- Do I need design skills? No, but layout awareness helps.
- What’s most important? Clear offer and proof; strong headline + CTA.
- How do I price? Tie to scope and deliverables; avoid per-hour.
- Can beginners get results? Yes—with clean structure and testing.
