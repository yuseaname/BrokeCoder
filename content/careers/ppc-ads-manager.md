# PPC / Ads Manager (Google/Meta)

Hook: Turn ad spend into measurable outcomes—structured testing, clear reporting.

## What You Do
You plan, launch, and optimize paid campaigns. You design structure, choose targeting, test creatives, and report performance honestly.

### Daily Work
- Build campaigns and ad groups with clean naming
- Research audiences and keywords
- Write ad copy and coordinate creatives
- Monitor CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS; iterate quickly
- Report learnings and next steps

### Deliverables
- Account structure and naming conventions
- Creative brief with copy angles + assets
- Weekly performance report and recommendations
- Testing roadmap (hypotheses, variants, metrics)

## Who This Is Good For
- Analytical marketers who enjoy iteration
- Writers/design-collaborators who like performance data

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Platform skills: Google Ads, Meta Ads
- Targeting and conversion tracking setup
- Landing page alignment and CRO basics
- Budget pacing and bid strategies

### Soft Skills
- Communication and honest reporting
- Process rigor and documentation
- Hypothesis-driven testing mindset

## Tools You’ll Use
- Google Ads, Meta Ads, Google Tag Manager
- GA4, Looker/Data Studio, Sheets dashboards
- Creative tools: Figma, Canva

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Learn account structure and naming conventions
- Set up conversion tracking; verify with test conversions
- Launch small-budget campaigns to practice

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Create a testing framework (hooks, angles, formats)
- Run 3 experiments with documented outcomes
- Build a performance dashboard (ROAS, CPA)

### 60–90 Days (Client-Ready)
- Offer pilot campaigns for 2 small businesses
- Deliver honest reporting; propose next-step tests
- Gather case studies and testimonials

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Campaign build with clean structure + results
- Creative testing case study (angles + CTR changes)
- Attribution and tracking setup with validation
- End-to-end funnel audit with CRO suggestions

## How to Get Your First Client/Job
1. Practice with small budgets (your brand or friends)
2. Publish transparent case studies—wins and misses
3. Pitch 20 businesses with audit + test plan
4. Offer pilot with capped budget and clear goals

## Pricing & Salary Ranges (No Guarantees)
- Employment: $55k–$95k entry; $80k–$130k mid-level
- Freelance retainers: $800–$4,000/mo (varies by scope)
- Setup packages: $600–$2,500 account build + tracking

> Disclaimer: Ad performance depends heavily on the offer, creative quality, traffic quality, and budget sufficiency. No guarantees.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- No tracking → Verify events, pixels, and conversion goals
- One-size targeting → Test audiences and keywords systematically
- Random creatives → Use briefs; test hooks and angles
- No landing page alignment → Fix message match; improve speed and UX

## FAQs
- How much budget do I need? Enough to test meaningfully (varies by niche).
- What’s most important? Offer quality and testing discipline.
- Can beginners get clients? Yes—start small, be transparent, iterate.
