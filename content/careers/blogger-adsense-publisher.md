# Blogger / AdSense Publisher

Hook: Share useful content; earn from display ads as traffic grows.

## What You Do
You publish helpful articles, attract search traffic, and monetize with display ads.

## Day in the Life
- Morning: outline a post; gather examples and images
- Midday: draft, add headings, tables, FAQs, and internal links
- Afternoon: publish and promote; review analytics and RPMs
- Weekly: update 2–3 older posts; refine layouts for CTR/RPM

### Daily Work
- Plan topics and write drafts
- Optimize posts and add visuals
- Track traffic and RPMs
- Update and re-link older posts

### Deliverables
- Content calendar and topic clusters
- 20–50 helpful posts over months
- Analytics dashboards and RPM tracking

Extended deliverables:
- CTR improvement plan (titles, images, layout)
- RPM experiments (ad placement changes vs UX)
- Pillar pages and hub navigation
- Newsletter plan for consistent readership

## Who This Is Good For
- Writers who enjoy consistency and research
- Builders patient with compounding results

## Skills You Need
### Hard Skills
- Keyword research and helpful writing
- On-page SEO and internal linking
- Basic analytics and RPM optimization

### Soft Skills
- Discipline and long-term focus
- Reader empathy and clarity

## Tools You’ll Use
- CMS (WordPress/Webflow)
- Analytics (GSC, GA4)
- Ad networks (AdSense, Ezoic, Mediavine)

Optional: image compressors, table generators, and schema tools for FAQ/Article.

## Learning Roadmap (0–90 Days)
### 0–30 Days (Foundation)
- Launch site with 10 helpful posts
- Build internal links and categories
- Set up analytics and speed optimizations

### 30–60 Days (Portfolio + Systems)
- Publish 20 more posts; add visuals
- Improve CTR with titles and images
- Apply for ad networks

### 60–90 Days (Expansion-Ready)
- Update older posts; add FAQs and links
- Publish pillar pages and hub content
- Build newsletter for retention

Add one RPM study: test ad density and placement against bounce rate—optimize for reader trust first.

## Portfolio Projects (3–6)
- Topic cluster with metrics
- “From zero to traffic” case study
- RPM improvements and layouts

Acceptance criteria examples:
- Cluster: hub and supports interlinked; traffic rises on long-tails
- Case study: honest timeline and improvements (CTR, time on page)
- RPM layout: clean UX; improved earnings without hurting engagement

## How to Get Your First Income
1. Publish 30+ posts across stable topics
2. Apply to ad networks
3. Optimize layouts for RPM and UX

### Monetization Models
- Display ads first; affiliate later for select posts
- Newsletter and simple digital products if helpful

## Income Ranges (No Guarantees)
- $50–$1,000+/mo as traffic grows
- Highly variable; compounding over time

> Disclaimer: No guaranteed income. Results depend on traffic, niche, and RPM.

## Common Mistakes & Fixes
- Thin posts → Add examples, steps, FAQs
- Slow site → Optimize images; use caching; simplify themes
- No updates → Refresh content quarterly

Additional:
- Ad overload → Respect readers; prioritize helpfulness and clarity
- Weak headings → Use scannable H2/H3 structure; answer intent early

## FAQs
- How long till traffic? Often months; depends on niche and quality.
- Do images matter? Yes—improve scannability and CTR.
- Is niche selection important? Yes—choose stable topics.
- How many posts per week? 2–4 steady; maintain quality
- Should I use AI? Assist for outlines; write and edit carefully
